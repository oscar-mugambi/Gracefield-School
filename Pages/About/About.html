Our Mission
Provide quality education that lays the right foundation for your child to achieve their full potential.
Our values are - caring and nurturing; highly professional, devoted and committed; excelling in all we do; trustworthy.

Our Staff
Our teaching team has a diverse background. We are experienced teachers, who have worked at Greengates Kindergarten and at International Schools in Washington D.C., Dar es Salaam, and Nairobi. We believe in working as a team, together with parents and guardians, towards achieving excellence. And we believe in providing a well-rounded education, one that covers all areas of childhood development.
