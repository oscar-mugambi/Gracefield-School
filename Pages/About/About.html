Our School
Our kindergarten is located in the Lavington suburb of Nairobi. It is set in a large compound that is serene, secure and with plenty of space to learn and play.
Our mission is to provide a quality, holistic education that lays the right foundation for your child to achieve their full potential.
We strive to provide our services with passion, professionalism and integrity.
Our classrooms are spacious, warm and friendly. They offer a conducive environment for young minds to develop and learn.

Our Staff
Our teaching team comes from a diverse background. We have experienced teachers who have worked at renowned schools such as Greengates Kindergarten as well as other International schools outside the country.

We believe in working as a team with our parents and guardians towards achieving excellence.
