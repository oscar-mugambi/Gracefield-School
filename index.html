Explore and Learn Welcome to Gracefield Kindergarten.
Our focus is providing a solid, well-rounded foundation.
We treat each child as an individual, nurturing their interests and talents. Our children are prepared to qualify for enrollment at all leading schools in Kenya and beyond. Our kindergarten’s programme is structured around the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS), the first stage of the British National Curriculum.
Please get in touch with us to arrange a visit. We would be happy to show you around the school.
